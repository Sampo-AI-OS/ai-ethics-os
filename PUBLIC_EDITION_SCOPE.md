# Public Edition Scope

This folder contains the curated public-edition version of AI Ethics OS for the Sampo AI OS portfolio.

It should also be understood as a public Sampo AI OS showcase repository: a clean, reviewable example of how the broader platform is presented in public.

## Included

This public edition is intended to demonstrate:

- the backend architecture
- EU AI Act-oriented scoring and guidance logic
- evidence-chain and certificate concepts
- demo frontend behavior
- test and documentation quality
- a scoring-first public product slice rather than the entire internal implementation

## Intentionally Excluded

The original internal working project may contain additional private materials that are not included here, such as:

- opportunity analysis
- internal planning
- business positioning
- sales framing
- other commercially sensitive strategy artifacts
- the full live audit runner
- the detailed internal scenario library and evaluator content
- other technically distinctive components that carry disproportionate commercial value

## Why This Matters

This repository edition is designed to be public-facing and portfolio-safe. It should be understood as a curated showcase, not as a full dump of the original working environment.

In practice, that means this edition does not publish every technically interesting component. Some parts were intentionally withheld because they function as differentiated product IP rather than portfolio proof.

## Branding

AI Ethics OS is presented here as a project powered by Sampo AI OS.

In portfolio terms, this repository acts as a Sampo AI OS shop window: it should be technically strong, easy to evaluate, and clearly presented as a showcase rather than an active collaboration workspace.
